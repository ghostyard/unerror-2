i’m k, my pronouns are she/they, i’m 25, my time zone’s pst, and there’s a lot to say here, so let’s get into it.

CONTENT WARNING this blog will have a lot of triggering content, including but not limited to: mental illness (specifically dissociative identity disorder, depression, and anxiety), violence, drug usage, trauma, and current events. i tag for all of this as trigger tw, which translates as mental illness tw, drugs tw, etc. because of this, i will not write with minors.

ACTIVITY this is a low activity blog. i will be on and off here as my brain works, and if i’m not here, you might find me at @debtbound.

SHIPPING i can ship, but i’m very careful about who i do and don’t ship with. elliot is, again, a lot for himself and even more of a handful for other people, so anything we do will have to be plotted out.

WRITING & STYLE i don't use icons and i use normal sized text. i don't mind if other people use small text. double small text can be hard for me to read sometimes. also, although i generally write in third person, i like first person threads. you'll never meet a guy more befitting of first person threads than elliot alderson.

ON CANON i basically follow the canon of the show. there’s very little that i change besides some notes that you can see in the portrayal section on this page.

INTERACTIONS elliot by his nature basically demands plotting before interacting. ask memes can also be very helpful for me, since it lets me mess around in his head about a person before getting into something. i may occasionally put out starter calls, but they won’t be as frequent on this blog as other blogs.

NEED A SUMMARY? i genuinely recommend reading the wiki summaries of the show. if you want to chat about anything or have questions, let me know!

001 elliot is not a particularly nice person. he’s trying to do good, but he is hampered by paranoia and severe mental illness. even in post-canon, when it's more controlled, it is a long and difficult uphill battle. he will lie to your muse. he will dissociate and talk to the reader just to get the hell out of there. he is often compelled to hack just about everyone he meets. he does not trust easily.

002 please be respectful about mental illness on here. there is no such thing as a 'bad' alter. every alter has their purpose and a reason that they needed to exist, regardless of how kind or agreeable they are.

003 i interpret elliot as a trans man. terfs and transphobes can fuck off. he uses he/him pronouns generally. this means that pronouns can sometimes be a little unclear on this blog. please note that "they" is often used to refer to the system as a whole.

004 i diverge from canon on a few things, more to fit my portrayal than any actual issues with canon. something important to note is that each of his parents had pre-existing marriages. elliot was his mother's biological child, and when they were married, he was around two or three years old and darlene had just been born to his father's prior marriage. this technically makes them adoptive siblings.

005 plotting during the canon of mr. robot can be incredibly difficult. all 45 episodes of its show happen across, basically, march 2015 to december 2015. elliot is basically not available for significant portions of time during that. it's easiest to plot with them post-canon, and some things might be placed pre-canon.
