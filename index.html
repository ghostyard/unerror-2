CONTENT WARNING this blog will have a lot of triggering content, including but not limited to: mental illness (specifically dissociative identity disorder, depression, and anxiety), violence, drug usage, trauma, and current events. i tag for all of this as trigger tw, which translates as mental illness tw, drugs tw, etc. because of this, i will not write with minors.

ACTIVITY this is a low activity blog. i will be on and off here as my brain works, and if i’m not here, you might find me at @debtbound.

SHIPPING i can ship, but i’m very careful about who i do and don’t ship with. elliot is, again, a lot for himself and even more of a handful for other people, so anything we do will have to be plotted out.

WRITING & STYLE i don't use icons and i use normal sized text. i don't mind if other people use small text. double small text can be hard for me to read sometimes. also, although i generally write in third person, i like first person threads. you'll never meet a guy more befitting of first person threads than elliot alderson.

INTERACTIONS elliot by his nature basically demands plotting before interacting. ask memes can also be very helpful for me, since it lets me mess around in his head about a person before getting into something. i may occasionally put out starter calls, but they won’t be as frequent on this blog as other blogs.

Hello, friend.

Every day we change the world.

imagine a world like ours, but a few seconds closer to midnight.

the world of mr. robot is barely outside of the one we live in. it's a little grimier, a little angrier, and a lot more prone to revolution. it's got economic collapse, rampant capitalism left so completely unfettered that it's formed enormous conglomerates, everyone drowning in debt and work.

it's about being so particularly and paralyzingly alone that living in the world becomes so untenable that it has to change. then it asks the question: what happens after you change the world, and everything keeps turning anyway, way outside your control?

this naturally fits into: crime, near-future sci-fi, urban fantasy, certain kinds of horror. anything else is workable. you'd be shocked how easy it is to find an analogue for hacking in most settings.

themes: neo-noir, technology, social isolation, revolution, anti-capitalism, trauma, and mental illness.

MAJOR PLAYERS

  • FSOCIETY - a secretive hacker collective formed in 2015 focused on the elimination of debt. the group took credit for 5/9, where all of e corp's debt records were encrypted, an incident in which 71 e corp buildings were blown up, and a massive document dump on the one hundred richest people in the world.
  • E CORP - one of the largest multi-national conglomerates in the world who, prior to 5/9, owned 70% of the global consumer credit industry. still one of the world's most important corporations, although its reputation has been somewhat diminished.
  • DARK ARMY - a notorious worldwide hacker-for-hire collective, intensely secretive yet infamous. in truth, the dark army worked directly for whiterose as a tool of violence and intimidation, although it seems to have dissolved after its leader's death.
  • DEUS GROUP - an investment group formed by the world's one hundred richest people to manipulate global events for profit. in truth, this was another tool used by whiterose to construct towards a machine, established in washington township for the purposes of finding another world.

THE PUBLIC KNOWS

  • elliot alderson was associated with stopping the washington township nuclear plant from melting down over christmas 2015.
  • everyone else in the power plant died, including the leader of the infamous deus group, a collection of the hundred or so richest people in the world.
  • fsociety, a hacker collective, outed the deus group. they were also responsible for 5/9 in early 2015, which encrypted most debt records and tanked the economy. someone—likely fsociety—later fixed that by emailing the encryption key to e corp.
  • they're also associated with blowing up 71 buildings and killing thousands of people. there's a lot of talk about that one.

HACKERS KNOW

  • sepi0l is an infamous and nigh-invisible hacker who disappeared for most of 2015 and then resurfaced in late 2016.
  • no one really knows anything about the original members of fsociety. there was a secondary group that popped up after 5/9, but the footprint wasn't the same.
  • lots of rumors about fsociety in general. people say they were a part of it, but that might not be true. there's plenty of groups who popped up trying to be their successors, too.
  • in terms of work, sepi0l spends a lot of time on irc boards and has written some fucking unhinged malware. they love open source projects and they hate suits, whether government or business.

LAW ENFORCEMENT KNOWS

  • elliot alderson has a criminal record. he took a deal to not be tried for property damage, and later went to jail for computer hacking and stealing a guy's dog.
  • elliot alderson was investigated by the fbi in connection to fsociety, 5/9, and the 71 buildings. he wasn't charged. a lot of it seems to rest at the feet of now-deceased e corp cto tyrell wellick.
  • if you're at a high enough clearance level, you know more about the details. elliot's sister was briefly arrested in connection to fsociety. it appears that a lot of what happened was related to the dark army, a criminal hacking syndicate used by the deus group as their muscle.
